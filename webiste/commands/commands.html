NotePad voice Commands

Note:These commands on pad only work before starting Recognition,
while recognizing speech it does not work, Hit 'SpaceBar' to pause and then use voice command

  • say 'openpad': To open notepad
  • say 'Start': To Start Recognition
  • Hit 'SpaceBar': To Pause Recognition :Does not have a voice command
  • say 'save': To save Text
  • say 'Speak': To listen audio of the text in the notepad
  • say 'Go Back': To Go To Home Page

Music Commands

  • say 'openmusic': To open Music Player
  • say 'play': To play Music
  • say 'stop': To stop Music
  • say 'previous_song': To play previous_song
  • say 'next_song': To play next_song
  • say 'Go Back': To Go To Home Page
